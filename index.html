About the Workshop

Memory, Material & the Art of Keeping

Photographs rarely exist alone. They are often accompanied by letters, tickets, notes, and fragments of everyday life that carry personal meaning.

In this hands-on workshop, participants will transform these memories into sculptural keepsakes by encasing photographs and personal ephemera within crystal-clear resin forms. Each participant will create two unique resin objects while exploring memory, preservation, and storytelling through material.

What to bring: Participants are encouraged to bring copies of photographs and small personal items (up to 4 inches) such as letters, tickets, pressed flowers, or other paper memorabilia.

Please note: Resin requires approximately 24 hours to cure. Participants will take their moulds home and demould the final pieces the following day.
